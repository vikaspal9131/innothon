  • Getting Started

  • How do I use the ECG prediction form?

    To use the prediction form, simply upload your ECG signal file through the provided upload option. After uploading, you can submit the file for analysis. The system will process your data and provide a prediction along with relevant insights and suggestions based on the analysis.

  • What file formats are supported for upload?

    We support .mat files for ECG signal data. Please ensure your file is formatted correctly and adheres to the specified input shape for accurate predictions.

  • How long does it take to get a prediction?

    The processing time depends on the size of the ECG signal file and the complexity of the analysis. Typically, it takes a few minutes to generate a prediction and provide insights.

  • What information will I receive after submitting my data?

    After submission, you will receive a detailed report including prediction results, disease information, and suggestions for further action. Additionally, you can view data visualizations and compare predictions over time.

  • Can I upload multiple ECG signal files at once?

    Yes, you can upload multiple ECG signal files simultaneously. The system will process each file individually and provide separate predictions and insights for each.

  • Analysis Details

  • What kind of analysis is performed on the ECG signals?

    The analysis includes mathematical operations (mean, standard deviation, etc.), Fourier transform, filtering, smoothing, differentiation, and integration. We also provide disease predictions, confidence scores, and detailed analytics on the ECG signals.

  • What types of diseases can be diagnosed?

    Our system can diagnose multiple diseases based on the analysis of ECG signals. The specific diseases and conditions are detailed in the prediction report.

  • Can I compare previous predictions with new data?

    Yes, you can compare previous predictions with new data using our comparison table and visualizations available on the dashboard.

  • What kind of mathematical operations can be performed on the ECG signals?

    The system performs various mathematical operations including mean, standard deviation, range, interquartile range, kurtosis, and skewness, among others.

  • What kind of graphs and charts are available on the dashboard?

    The dashboard includes graphs and charts for machine learning metrics (accuracy, inference time, loss, etc.), comparison of previous predictions, and other data visualizations related to ECG signal analysis.
