Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in each plan?

Each plan comes with different levels of access and support. The Basic Plan includes essential features, the Standard Plan adds priority support and exclusive content, while the Premium Plan offers the most comprehensive package with unlimited storage and 24/7 support.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can easily upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. To do so, simply visit your account settings or contact our support team, and we will assist you with the process.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure payment methods. You can select your preferred payment option during the checkout process.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings on our website. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more details, and contact our support team if you need further assistance.

What happens if I miss a payment?

If a payment fails, your account may be suspended temporarily until the issue is resolved. You will receive notifications regarding the payment issue and instructions on how to fix it. Recurrent issues may lead to account deactivation.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for new users to experience our services. You can sign up for the trial without providing payment information, and you can choose to subscribe to a plan at the end of the trial period.
